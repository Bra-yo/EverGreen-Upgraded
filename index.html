    Our Services

    Evergreen Estates offers a comprehensive range of real estate services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our services include residential sales, property lettings, and expert property management. Whether you're looking to buy, sell, rent, or need professional management for your investment properties, our experienced team provides personalized support every step of the way. With a strong focus on customer satisfaction and a deep understanding of the local market, Evergreen Estates ensures a smooth, efficient, and stress-free real estate experience.

    Residential Sales

    Evergreen Estates helps clients buy and sell residential properties with ease. Our team provides expert advice, handles negotiations, and ensures smooth transactions, whether you're a first-time buyer or an experienced investor.

    Property Lettings

    We offer professional letting services for landlords and tenants. Our team assists in finding the right tenants for your property and ensures all legal requirements are met, offering peace of mind for both parties.

    Property Management

    Our property management service takes the stress out of managing your investment property. From tenant screenings and rent collection to property maintenance, we handle every aspect of property management efficiently.

    Valuations and Market Appraisals

    At Evergreen Estates, we provide accurate property valuations and market appraisals to help sellers, buyers, and investors make informed decisions. Our expert team uses local market knowledge to give you the most up-to-date insights.

    Investment Consultancy

    We offer consultancy services for property investors, helping them find the best opportunities based on their goals. Whether you’re looking for residential or commercial properties, we guide you through the process of building and managing a profitable portfolio.

    Commercial Property Services

    Evergreen Estates also specializes in commercial real estate, assisting businesses with buying, selling, or leasing office spaces, retail outlets, and industrial properties. We ensure the process is tailored to your specific business needs.

    Our Agents
